BRIEFING & DESIGN

Zones' Role is to work with you to design and deliver the best project you can achieve with your budget. We will translate your requirements into landscape design for you to review. During this stage, a budget indication range will be provided to avoid any nasty surprises in the build stage.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Landscaping is a complicated process and trades need to be constantly re-scheduled to ensure the project is not held up. Your Zones project manager will ensure work is completed to the right standards at each stage so there are no quality issues or disputes at the end of the project.

MAINTAIN
